What is Buffalo Boost 716?

Built for Speed.
Designed for Everyone.

Buffalo Boost 716 is WNY's first indoor RC car racing experience — powered by FPV technology that makes you feel like you're actually behind the wheel. From our founding members to our opening day, every detail is being built from the ground up right here in Western New York.
