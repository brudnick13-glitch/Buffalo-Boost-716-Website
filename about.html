Our Story

About Buffalo Boost 716

Buffalo Boost 716 is WNY's first indoor RC car racing experience — built for speed, designed for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned hobbyist or picking up a controller for the first time, we put you behind the wheel of a 1/10 scale Traxxas RC car and inside the action through first-person view (FPV) goggles that make you feel like you're actually on the track.

"Our 8 FPV simulator stations pair professional-grade HDZero goggles with Logitech steering wheels, delivering an immersive racing experience unlike anything else in the region."

We have many exciting upcoming projects we can not wait to show to you. We're slowly building our operation and with your support we will be there in no time.

Buffalo Boost 716 was built from the ground up using cutting-edge AI tools to engineer the technical systems, plan the business, and bring the concept to life, design, and implement changes faster than traditional methods would allow. We're proud to be on the leading edge — not just in racing, but in how we build.

This is just the beginning. Coming soon to Western New York.
